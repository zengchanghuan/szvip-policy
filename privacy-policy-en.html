Privacy Policy

Updated: July 22, 2026 | Effective: July 22, 2026

Shenzhen VIP Technology Co., Ltd. Privacy Policy

This app is mainly used for local interaction with gimbal hardware and does not rely on an account system or cloud account service. Captured media is processed locally unless you explicitly enable diagnostics.

1. Data processed locally

  • Bluetooth connection status and device connection results
  • Media and files you create in the app (stored locally)
  • Basic permission state required to enable features
  • Capture location when you enable Location Information, written only to photo metadata and stored on the device
  • Device-orientation sensor results for interface rotation, processed only on the device

2. Optional diagnostic data sharing

We initialize the Alibaba Cloud Mobile Monitoring SDK (CrashAnalysis and RemoteLog) only after you explicitly agree to the current Privacy Policy and enable Diagnostic Data Sharing.

  • Provider: Hangzhou Alibaba Cloud Intelligence Technology Co., Ltd.
  • Purpose: minimize crashes, improve performance, diagnose Bluetooth, camera, and video-processing issues, and provide customer support
  • Data: network type; OS version, CPU architecture, device brand/model, screen resolution and language; carrier name and country; app version/channel; crash stacks, handled errors and performance diagnostics; product interactions such as feature taps and mode changes; redacted operational logs; an app-generated anonymous installation identifier; and an SDK-generated device identifier
  • Processing: crashes and errors may upload automatically; operational logs are cached on the device and may be remotely retrieved by authorized support personnel; you may also upload them from Feedback & Help

Alibaba Cloud states that Crash Analysis may use MAC and IP addresses to distinguish a physical device from a simulator, but does not store or upload them. Diagnostics never upload photo, video, or audio content.

Alibaba Cloud EMAS Privacy Policy: official policy

3. Linking and tracking

Diagnostics are linked to an anonymous installation or device identifier so faults from the same installation can be grouped. We do not use this data for advertising profiles, cross-app tracking, or advertising measurement, and we do not sell or share it with data brokers.

4. Permissions explanation

  • Bluetooth: used to connect gimbal hardware
  • Camera / Microphone: used only when you actively capture photo/video/audio
  • Storage / Media access: used to save and read local media files

5. Sharing and protection

Except for the Alibaba Cloud EMAS processing described above, legal requirements, or protecting users and the public, we do not share diagnostics with third parties. We require service providers to protect the data to the same or a higher standard than this policy and applicable law.

6. Retention and deletion

Local media remains on your device until you delete it. Local operational logs rotate at no more than five files of 512 KB each, and no more than ten MetricKit diagnostic files are retained. Cloud diagnostics are retained only for the minimum period needed to investigate faults and meet legal obligations, then deleted or anonymized according to the EMAS console retention configuration.

7. Your choices and rights

When the Privacy Policy is first shown, you can decline diagnostic data sharing and continue using basic camera, recording, and gimbal controls. You can later enable or withdraw consent at any time under Me - Diagnostic Data Sharing. Withdrawal stops diagnostics and clears diagnostic files stored by the app.

You may use the email below to request access to, correction of, or deletion of cloud diagnostics. We may need the anonymous installation identifier or other necessary information to locate the records and will respond within the period required by applicable law.

8. Minors

If you are a minor, use this app under guardian supervision.

9. Policy updates

If the data types, purposes, processing methods, or third parties materially change, we will update the policy version and present it again before continuing the affected processing.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact shown in the app.
