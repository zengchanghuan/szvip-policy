1. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

1.1 Categories of Information Collected

To provide you with our services, we will require you to provide personal information necessary to provide you with the services. If you do not provide personal information, we may not be able to provide you with our products or services.

We collect the following categories of information (whether or not they constitute personal information):

  • Information you provide to us or upload (including your contact information). We may collect any or all personal information you provide to us.
  • Device-related information: Information related to your device. For example, device model, device firmware version number, sensor information, hardware serial number.
  • Application-related information: Information related to your application. For example, software version number, client type.
  • Location information (only applicable to specific services/functions): Information related to your location. For example, when searching for nearby Bluetooth devices, we need to obtain location permissions.
  • Image and video information: To enable you to view and operate within the software, the photos and videos you take will be stored locally.
  • Recording information: To better use video services with recording functions, the audio and video information you record will be saved to your phone.

When you access our web pages through a computer, or access our mobile web pages through a mobile smart terminal, download and use our or our affiliated companies' client software to use our services, we may read information related to your device, such as Media Access Control (MAC) address, browser name and version, language, geographic location information, device manufacturer and model, device identification code, operating system, resolution, telecommunications operator, etc.

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Provide, process, maintain, improve, develop our products and/or services provided to you, including after-sales service and customer support, as well as services provided through devices or websites.
  • Communicate with you regarding your devices, services or any general inquiries (such as updates, customer consultation support, information about our activities, notifications).
  • Analyze and develop statistics related to the use of our products and services to better improve our products and services.
  • Store and maintain information related to you for our business operations or to fulfill legal obligations.

More details on how we use your information (which may include personal information):

  • Send notifications: We may from time to time use your personal information to send important notifications, such as promotional notifications about related accessory activities, user offline activity notifications, and changes to our terms, conditions and policies.
  • Provide location-based services: We or third-party service providers may obtain location permissions to discover nearby Bluetooth devices to provide you with services and provide accurate locations.
  • View and manage your images and videos: You can access, view and delete photos and videos taken by the APP. The photos and videos you take are only limited to local access within the VIP handheld phone gimbal software, and we do not store them.
  • Provide recording services: We provide recording permission application services based on your use of the video recording function to ensure the use of the recording function.
  • Device sensors: When you use our product services, we will call the device's gravity sensor to identify and determine your device status.
  • Acceleration sensor: Obtain acceleration sensor information to achieve app horizontal and vertical screen switching UI effects.
