Privacy Policy

Updated: May 19, 2026

This app is designed primarily for local interaction with gimbal hardware and does not rely on account systems or cloud services.

1. Information We Process

  • Device and application information (model, OS version, app version, crash diagnostics)
  • Bluetooth connection status and device pairing results
  • Media and files generated within the app (stored locally on your device)
  • Basic permission states required to enable features

2. Purpose of Processing

  • Connect and control gimbal hardware via Bluetooth
  • Provide photo, video, preview, and local media management features
  • Ensure app stability and resolve failures

3. Permissions

  • Bluetooth: Used to connect gimbal hardware
  • Camera / Microphone: Used only when you actively capture photo or video
  • Storage / Media access: Used to save and read local media files

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is only processed by compliant third-party SDKs to the minimum extent necessary when required by law or to ensure app stability.

5. Storage and Retention

Local media is managed by you and removed when deleted. Diagnostic data is retained only for the minimum period required by law and operations.

6. Your Choices

You may deny or revoke permissions in system settings; however, the corresponding features may become unavailable.

7. Minors

Minors should use this app under the guidance of a parent or guardian.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the in-app support channel.
