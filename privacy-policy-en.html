VIP Technology Co., Ltd. Privacy Policy

This app is mainly used for local interaction with gimbal hardware and does not rely on an account system or cloud account service.

1. Data we process

  • Device and app info (model, OS version, app version, crash diagnostics)
  • Bluetooth connection status and device connection results
  • Media and files you create in the app (stored locally)
  • Basic permission state required to enable features

2. Why we process it

  • Connect to and control gimbal hardware via Bluetooth
  • Provide camera, recording, preview, and local media management features
  • Maintain app stability and fix crashes

3. Permissions explanation

  • Bluetooth: used to connect gimbal hardware
  • Camera / Microphone: used only when you actively capture photo/video/audio
  • Storage / Media access: used to save and read local media files

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only process/share data when required by law, or when a compliant third-party SDK is strictly necessary for app stability.

5. Data retention

Local media remains on your device until you delete it. Diagnostic data retention follows legal and operational minimum requirements.

6. Your choices

You can deny or revoke permissions in system settings, but related features may become unavailable.

7. Minors

If you are a minor, use this app under guardian supervision.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact shown in the app.
