Privacy Policy — Gimbal View

Effective: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gimbal View ("we", "us", or "our"), published on Google Play by Shenzhen VIP Technology Co., Ltd., handles information when you use our mobile gimbal control app on Android. The policy applies to the Gimbal View app distributed on Google Play; it does not cover any third-party services or websites you may access from the app.

Summary

• We do not collect any personal data. Gimbal View works entirely on your device.

• No accounts, no sign-up, no servers. You do not need to register to use the app.

• Nothing is sent off your device. Photos, videos, and recordings stay on your phone.

• No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

• We do not sell or share your personal information.

1. Information We Process

Gimbal View does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us. The app does not maintain user accounts and we operate no servers that receive user data.

The information below is processed only locally on your device and is never sent to us or any third party:

Type How it is used Where it stays
Camera (video) and microphone (audio) Live preview and recording through the gimbal Your device storage
Photos and videos Saved to your device gallery for your own viewing and management Your device storage
Bluetooth Discover and connect to the gimbal hardware Your device (Bluetooth stack)
Location (approximate) Required by Android to scan for nearby Bluetooth devices. Gimbal View does not use your location for any other purpose. Your device only; never stored or sent
Motion sensors (gravity / accelerometer) Detect device orientation for UI rotation and gimbal control Processed in real time; not stored

2. Permissions We Request

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in Android Settings → Apps → Gimbal View → Permissions. Some features may stop working if a required permission is revoked.

Permission Why Gimbal View needs it Required
Camera Preview and record video through the gimbal Yes
Microphone Record audio together with your videos Optional
Photos / Storage Save and view captured photos and videos in your local gallery Yes
Bluetooth Pair with and control the gimbal hardware Yes
Location (approximate) Required by Android in order to scan for Bluetooth devices. Not used for any other purpose. Yes (required by Android)

3. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to share or sell.

We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • Share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers.
  • Integrate any third-party SDK that collects personal information.

If we are ever compelled by valid legal process (for example, a court order), we may disclose any information we are legally required to provide. Because we do not store user data, we will normally have no user records to disclose.

4. Third-Party Services

Gimbal View currently does not integrate any third-party SDK that collects, stores, or shares your personal information.

If we add such services in the future (for example, optional crash reporting or analytics), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy and list the service in this section.
  • Update our Google Play Data safety disclosures accordingly.
  • Make any sensitive integration optional or clearly notify you within the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Gimbal View is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect any data at all, this protection applies automatically. If you are a parent or guardian and have any concern, please contact us using the address in section 8.

6. Data Security

Since all data stays on your device, the security of your information depends on the security of your phone. We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device and operating system up to date.
  • Use a screen lock and biometric authentication.
  • Review app permissions regularly in Android Settings.

To remove all Gimbal View-related data, simply uninstall the app from your device.
